## About The Project

People use other people's services every day. They call to book an appointment or spend a lot of time to do it face to face. They rarely have a chance to make an appointment via the internet. Do you sometimes feel overwhelmed by keeping an eye on a lot of different appointments and manually entering them into your calendar? I do, and that's why the idea of an application connecting entrepreneurs with clients was born in my head.

SeeMe is the fully secured solution for clients who want to have all their appointments in one place and service providers looking for new contacts. It's easy. Create an account, enter personal data and make your life easier. As a entrepreneur you can add your terms and manage them. Clients get unique possibility to look for needed specialist and govern their appointments.
